Typography

Heading Font: [Georgia, Serif]I chose a Serif font for my heading, for the same reasoning I picked a sans-serif for the paragraph font, because the difference between the two gives high contrast. This makes it easier to decihper and read both sets of text. Paragraph Font: [Helvetica, sans-serif] I chose a Sans-Serif font for my paragraph, for the same reasoning I picked a Serif for the heading font, because of the higher contrast between the two. I specifically picked a Sans-Serif for the paragraph because it's easier to read at smaller sizes.
