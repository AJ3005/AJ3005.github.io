Target audience

My target audience is people interested in the weather in the region and who may not know the area that well. This audience can range in age, but typically they will be around 20-50 years of age. these customers should be financially stable. My audience should be able to access my phone on all types of clients (desktop, laptop, mobile device, and tablets).
