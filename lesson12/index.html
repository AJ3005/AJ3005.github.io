The Foundation was originally incorporated in 1973 as the Chicago Association of Commerce and Industry Foundation. In 1998, the name was changed to the Chicagoland Chamber of Commerce Foundation. A rich history of leadership and business advocacy combined with the power of people, the Chamber continues to foster a dynamic economy, deliver value to members, and make Chicagoland a world-class place to live and work.
